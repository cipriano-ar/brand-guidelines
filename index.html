Logo

The Street Jackets logo is the key element of our Brand identity. It highlights vibrant colours that speak to the freshest ingredients in every meal we prepare.

It is designed to convey a clear and simple message: Street Jackets provides quick, delicious, high quality fresh food in a fun, upbeat atmosphere.

Logo

The Street Jackets logo is an arrangement of six basic elements:

  1. A set of knife and fork
  2. A pair of squeezy bottles
  3. A green circle
  4. The Street Jackets name
  5. Three red stars
  6. A black background

Our 4-colour logo has a great visual impact and should be always used on a black background.

All six elements combine nicely to produce that feeling of freshness, great flavour, simplicity, and quality.

Fonts

Another key element in brand identity and brand presence, is the font chosen to display our written words.

For our logo typeface we used the Swash and Silhouette fonts which were combined to give that catchy and funny western / country look.
